# Affiliate Marketing Starter Kit

## Quick Start Guide

### Step 1: Choose Your Niche
- Pick something you genuinely enjoy
- Check competition level on Google
- Look for products with affiliate programs

### Step 2: Find Affiliate Programs
- Amazon Associates (easiest start)
- ClickBank (digital products)
- ShareASale (various niches)
- CJ Affiliate (bigger brands)
- Direct brand programs (search "[brand] affiliate program")

### Step 3: Build Your Platform
- **Beginner:** Free website on WordPress.com or Wix
- **Intermediate:** Self-hosted WordPress with affiliate plugins
- **Content first:** Blog posts, reviews, comparison articles

### Step 4: Create Content
- Product reviews (honest, thorough)
- "Best X for Y" roundup posts
- How-to guides featuring products
- Tutorials with embedded links

### Step 5: Drive Traffic
- SEO (long-term, free)
- Pinterest (visual products)
- YouTube reviews
- Email list building
- Paid ads (once profitable)

## Essential Tools

### Tracking & Analytics
- Google Analytics (free)
- Bitly or Pretty Links (link tracking)

### Content Research
- Ubersuggest (keywords)
- AnswerThePublic (questions)

### Email Marketing
- MailerLite (free up to 1,000 subscribers)
- ConvertKit (creator-focused)

## Affiliate Disclosure Template
> "This post contains affiliate links. If you buy through my link, I may earn a commission at no extra cost to you."

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
1. Promoting too many products
2. Not disclosing affiliate relationships
3. Promoting low-quality products
4. Giving up too early (takes 6-12 months)
5. Not building an email list

## Best Niches for Beginners
- Personal finance (budgeting, investing)
- Health & fitness
- Technology & gadgets
- Pet supplies
- Home organization
- Digital products

## Income Expectations
- Month 1-3: $0-50 (building content)
- Month 3-6: $50-200 (early traction)
- Month 6-12: $200-1,000 (compounding)
- Year 2+: $1,000+ (scaling)
