# Reels Script Generator

Generate scroll-stopping scripts in seconds. Fill in the blanks.

---

## How It Works

1. Choose your category
2. Fill in the blanks
3. Add your hook
4. Record & post

---

## Categories

### Motivation/Quotes
**Template:**
```
[Hook - attention grabber]

[Quote or statement]

[Explanation or story]

[Call to action - like, follow, save]
```

**Example:**
```
Stop searching for motivation.

"Success is the sum of small efforts repeated day in and day out."

Most people quit on day 3.
Don't be most people.

Save this. Share with someone who needs it.
```

---

## Hooks That Work

- "Stop doing this if you want [result]"
- "Nobody talks about this but..."
- "The truth about [topic]"
- "I tried [thing] for 30 days - here's what happened"
- "POV: When you realize [truth]"
- "You're not [result] because of [reason]"
- "Save this for later"

---

## Call to Actions

- "Save this post"
- "Follow for more"
- "Share with a friend"
- "Comment below"
- "Like if you agree"
- "Turn on post notifications"

---

## Pro Tips

- Keep scripts 30-60 seconds
- Front-load value (first 3 seconds)
- End with engagement
- Use power words
- Be specific, not generic
