## Niche Selection Criteria

### What to look for:
- ✅ High search volume
- ✅ Engagement potential
- ✅ Ad revenue potential
- ✅ Product opportunities
- ✅ Easy to create content

### Red flags:
- ❌ Too saturated
- ❌ Hard to monetize
- ❌ Requires real face
- ❌ Hard to source content
- ❌ Controversial/risky

---

## How to Choose

**Answer these questions:**

1. What are you interested in?
2. What can you talk about for hours?
3. What would you learn about for fun?
4. What's monetizable?
5. Can you create content without showing face?

**Pick 1-2 niches. Master them. Then expand.**

---

## Content Mix Strategy

**70% - Your niche content**
Educational, entertaining, valuable

**20% - Engagement content**
Questions, polls, duets

**10% - Trending content**
Challenges, sounds, hashtags

---

## Growth Tips

1. Post 1-3 times daily
2. Use trending sounds early
3. Engage in comments
4. Collaborate with others
5. Repurpose across platforms
